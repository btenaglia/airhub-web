AIR FREIGHT

Allies Air is Nantucket's premier cargo shipment service between Hyannis and the island.

Conveniently located at our own freight handling facilities at the Nantucket Memorial Airport and Barnstable Municipal Airport, Allies Air takes the time and stress away from having to fight the chaotic ferry crowds when sending cargo.

Offering three daily trips, Allies Air is the most efficient and economical way to send your valuable business and/or personal items between the Island and Cape Cod.
